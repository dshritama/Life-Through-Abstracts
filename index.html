Hi, I am Shritama Das and let me begin with why I launched this blog.

Nothing is more abstract than life itself. Pure abstract art is an expression of your thoughts and your feelings. Your art is therefore a reflection of your own life and what you imagine it to be. Hence, I tried to paint impressions of my life and my feelings on the soft unmolded canvas. Here’s to unleashing our imaginations!

Hello Again!

My art, I believe, is inspired from the chaos within. It is difficult to be tamed in a cage of mere words because it represents my abstract thoughts and emotions. However, since art is but the convergence of various interpretations, feel free to predict it, the way you see and feel it.
